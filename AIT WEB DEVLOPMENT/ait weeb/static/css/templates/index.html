Key Features

Discover how NEUROGLOVE empowers users with cutting-edge technology

Gesture Control

Intuitive hand gestures translate into digital commands, allowing users to control devices with natural movements.

Health Monitoring

Real-time tracking of vital signs including temperature and pulse rate for proactive health management.

GPS Tracking

Integrated location tracking provides peace of mind for caregivers and ensures quick assistance when needed.

Home Automation

Control household devices, lights, and appliances with simple hand gestures for enhanced independence.

AI Voice Generator

Convert gestures to natural speech, enabling non-verbal users to communicate effectively with others.

Emergency Alerts

Immediate distress signal capability connecting users to emergency services and designated caregivers.
